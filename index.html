An Intimate Evening With Don Miguel Ruiz Jr. Friday, August 16, 2019 7:00 PM 9:00 PM InSpirit Center for Spiritual Living 25782 Obrero Drive Mission Viejo, CA 92691

This September, our Toltec community will come together from all over the country to recommit ourselves to the creation of a new dream —the Dream of Heaven on Earth. During this time, we invite friends, family and other like-minded people to join us in holding a beautiful intent for the planet — an intent of unconditional love. In the book Circle of Fire, don Miguel Ruiz inspires us to enter into a new and loving relationship with ourselves, with our fellow humans, and with all of creation. Through the work we prepare our minds for a new way of seeing life, and opening our hearts to find our way back to our birthright: heaven on earth. The result is a life lived in joy, harmony, and contentment. The Circle of Fire ceremony serves as an affirmation of our commitment to finding our integrity by listening to our hearts and not the distortions of our minds. It is a spiritual circle based in love, respect and honest communication. Participating in the Circle of Fire, you will join a group intent for personal freedom and gain a network of mutual support. We once again open the Circle of Fire to old and new friends and recommit ourselves to living from a place of truth and service. It is an opportunity for Toltecs to meet each other and support each other in changing the way we dream.

Friday, August 16, 2019: 7:00 PM - 9:00 PM
